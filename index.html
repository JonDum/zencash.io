Features

End-to-end Encryption

The Zen platform utilizes Zero-Knowledge Succinct Non-Interactive Argument of Knowledge (zk-SNARKS for short) to provide fully encrypted transactions that mask both the sender and receiver while still being fully provable and verifiable on the blockchain.

Built to last

Zen's mission is to be useful and used. To accomplish that, 12% of mining rewards go to the Zen Blockchain Foundation that then distributes funds to improve the codebase, wallet apps and market to new users. Furthermore, 30% of the treasuries funds automatically go to globally distributed secure nodes that ensure th network's health and speed. Within one month of announcement, over 6,000 independently operated secure nodes were launched around the globe with many more coming online every day.

Decentralized, Open Governance

Zen is not controlled by a single entity. Community members submit proposals to contribute to and support Zen, and, if the community accepts the proposal, get paid in ZEN for their effort.
